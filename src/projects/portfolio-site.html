Portfolio Site

Spring 2019 - Welcome to my portfolio site!



What inspired your site design?

I have nearly 2500 hours in Hexcells, a minesweeper game set in hexagons. Many of its visual elements inspired my website design: the color choice, thin font styling, and emphasis on minimalism.

I believe strongly in simple, intuitive, and interactive user interfaces.

Why from scratch?

My experience with Wix and SquareSpace was simple and short - I found the interface stifling.

The templates were certainly cool and organized. But I wanted a little more - I wanted to make something that would make people say "Wow, I've never seen that before."

"This site is easily my greatest project yet."
