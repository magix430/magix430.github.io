Piano Duet Arrangements - Sheet music available on Sheet.Host



What is Hollow Knight?

Hollow Knight is a metroidvania-style adventure game following a nondescript warrior bug through a kingdom plagued by a mysterious disease. The game is available on consoles (Switch, PS4, and Xbox One) and PC & Mac on Steam.

What did you make?

I arranged several soundtracks for piano duet in albums pertaining to battle music and ambience, named Mask and Soul, respectively. I have only made two arrangements so far (as preserving the mysticalness of the music is difficult): Dung Defender & City of Tears.
